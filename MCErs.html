Most Welcome to Mahama English Club

Mahama English Club was created since 2015 when Burundian refugees arrived in Easternn Province of Rwanda. It was a bad political climate happened in 2015 which caused these refugees to flee their coutry, Burundi to Rwanda. Most of them were young generations and they have wanted to keep on learning . They consider English as one of international languages beside French.They decided to learn English and formed a club named"Mahama English Club". It was so nice to see distinguished people(men, women, girls and boys) forming a beautiful club like this.

These are the students since 2015 until now. They were given different gifts like dictionaries, smart Phones, pens, and cupbooks by Anne Down Moretti, the sponsor of these young generations.

Below is the supporter, Anne Down Moretti from New York

She came from New York to Rwanda in 2015 and arrived in Mahama refugee Camp where refugee Burundians have lived.It is when she met this group of students.She loved them even their idea and tried to pivot and see all ways of helpping them.She gave them Smart Phones, uniforms,dictionaries, pens, and coppybooks. Some of these students are in Mahama Camp and others are studying at Davis College/Akilah in Kigali with the assistance of Anne Moretti.

"Each one teach one" and "Together we can"
