Our activities since 2015

Mahama English Club started its activities since 2015. The members of this club wanted to improve their skills by learning English as one among two international languages beside French. During this journey, they got a supportor, Anne Down Moretti who helped them achieving their ambitions. In 2016, she provided smart phones to some of Club members which helped them connect on different social media and learn effeciantly. In 2017, she pivoted and gave dictionaries, uniforms, and pens to 300 students who were tought by their fellow mentors. Each member of this club has to be on social media. This was her ambution. She came again from New York to Rwanda and provided 175 smart Phones to other round of club members. She kept on pivoting in different ways and provided more than 180 Smart Phones to the rest of Club members in October 2021. All club members are now on socail media. These actions done by mum helped students who were studying their high school to perform well in national exams. Some of them succeeded well in National exam and others attended different uninversities because of what she did for them. Bellow are pictures taken during those activities.
